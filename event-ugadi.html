About the event

At the foot of Arunachala Hill, Arunachaleswarar Temple is revered as the Fire element among Shiva's Pancha Bhoota Sthalams. A famous legend says Shiva once appeared here as an endless pillar of fire between Brahma and Vishnu, and that blazing jyoti later cooled into Arunachala Hill itself.
